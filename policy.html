Labour will:

-Extend paid parental leace from 18 weeks to 26 week.



Labour will pass the legistation to implement this within our first 100 days in Office. The policy will be phased in over three years: 22 weeks from 2018 and 26 weeks in 2020.

Getting to 26 weeks is an important goal. As well as the direct financial benefits to households, extending paid parental leave has a range of positive impacts in terms of child development and fostering parent-infant attachment. Extending Paid Parental Leave to twenty-six weeks (six months) would also align its duration with the World Health Organisation (WHO) recommendation of exclusive breastfeeding uo to six months of age.
