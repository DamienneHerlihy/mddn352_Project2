New Zealand First will:

- Cap the number of places at medical training institutions that can be taken by foreign students and increase the number for New Zealand students.

- Establish a national strategy for cancer, diabetes, osteoporosis, osteoarthritis, gout, asthma, obesity, and dementia.

- Require DHBs to provide health checks for all Year 9 students before they enter secondary school.

- Establish guaranteed maximum waiting times for a range of surgical and specialist treatment.
