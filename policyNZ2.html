Speak Up


New Zealand First will:

- Introduce a universal living allowance, and a student debt write-off scheme that would give students free tertiary study by requiring them to work in NZ for the same as their length of study.

- Abolish the charter or “partnership” school model, scrap National Standards in their current form and increase operations funding to schools.

- Restore the requirement for all early childhood education providers to have 100 per cent registered teachers.

- Review the special education funding model and budget to ensure support for all children and communities.
